College Board® approved online program

  • Our Presenters will be utilizing the following proposed format in their sessions:
    • One third (1/3) synchronous (group) learning
    • One third (1/3) asynchronous instruction (independent activity)
    • One third (1/3) individual learning or one to one with the Presenter
  • The daily scheduled hours will be the same: Monday through Friday, 8:00 am to 3:00 pm (Pacific Time) with time allotted for lunch/breaks.
  • The platform for instruction will be Google Classroom and Zoom.
  • Each participant will need a personal Gmail account to access the classroom session. Note: Many school Google classroom accounts do not allow access.
  • The Presenters will post their daily schedules when class begins.
  • College Board materials: All registrants with a U.S. Continental residence and International address will receive an electronic copy via email.

Scholarships

Please contact your A.P. Coordinator or visit the College Board website for further information and eligibility.

CEU Certificates

Within one day of the conclusion of the workshop, you will receive a certificate (via email) that states completion of a College Board endorsed (30 Contact Hours) Professional Development training in your subject area. If you are seeking to acquire CEU credits for your participation, this should meet the requirements.

Please note: The College Board has been approved as an authorized provider by the IACET (International Association for Continuing Education and Training). Please contact your district regarding its policy on accepting continuing education units as each district may have different guidelines.

Graduate Credit

We are offering Graduate Credit in the form of Extension Semester Units (ESU) or Continuing Education Units (CEU) from Dominican University of California; 2 units, $110 total.

Please contact your district regarding its policy on accepting professional and continuing education units as each district may have different guidelines.

Refunds

Refunds can be collected before July 1 with a $75.00 cancellation fee. After this date, a $100.00 fee will be retained and the remainder refunded upon request. July 25 is the deadline to cancel. After July 25, no refunds can be granted.

Course Cancellations

A subject area session may be cancelled due to lack of enrollment. In that case, a full refund will be issued.

Participant Attendance Policy

Fee still remains payable regardless of attendance record. It is the responsibility of the registrant and/or school to notify Southern California AP® Institute of the cancellation by July 25. If Southern California AP® Institute is not notified by July 25, the total fee is still applicable.

Confirmation

Each participant will receive a letter, via email, confirming a reserved seat in the session when payment is received.
