Since 1999, the Southern California AP® Institute has held an annual summer workshop in the beautiful locale of Palos Verdes, to equip high school AP® teachers with information that will enhance the achievement of their students on AP® exams in various subject matters.

Here's what you will receive in our program:

  • 30 hours of instruction
  • AP® materials from the College Board (all registrants will receive an electronic copy by email).
  • Review of the current exam
  • An opportunity to network with colleagues throughout the U.S. (and beyond)
  • Discussion on effectively teaching your subject matter
  • Course materials from presenters
  • Certificate of Merit (IACET College Board approved CEU Certificate)
  • Graduate Credit (2 units) ESU/CEU: Dominican University of California (additional fee)
  • Advice on how to plan your AP® calendar year and course audit
The 2024 online workshop will be conducted from August 5 through August 9. We look forward to your participation in our College Board-approved Online AP® Instruction program. Feel free to browse our curriculum and read about our faculty.
